About Us

Tao Expeditions has been described as ‘One of the most pioneering cultural eco tourism projects in the Philippines’.

It is a journey to the most remote region in Palawan to discover a tropical archipelago of hundreds of paradise islands, inaccessible to independent travelers through its remoteness and lack of tourist infrastructure.
